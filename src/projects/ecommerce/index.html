An Introduction

Whilst at blubolt I built the front-end for some of the UK's biggest eCommerce retailers. When I started at the company I was the only front-end developer by title so coded absolutely every site that left our doors! However, after a few years, I started to hire for, and managed the front-end team. This roll shift meant I oversaw smaller eCommerce builds, whilst still getting my hands dirty and coding the front-end for our bigger clients, but had more time to spend on internal projects and tooling. At at my time of leaving the company the front-end team was 6 people, all whom I am immensely proud of and will miss dearly.
