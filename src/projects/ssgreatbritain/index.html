Project

An interactive app for SS Great Britain that allows discovery of over 30,000 of the ship's inhabitants, both online and in the museum.

An Introduction

For one of my first projects at Deep Blue Sky; I was tasked with creating the front-end for an easy to use, interactive tablet experience for both online and in-museum kiosks. The project was many months of work. XXX

Technicals

The app was built using Vue.js, with all data coming from a custom API that our back-end team built. The CMS is Spirit, our in-house platform. The front-end is fully responsive and uses Google Maps for the maps functionality.

Impact

The app has had a big impact on-board the ship, launching to much praise from voluenteers and staff, as well as visitors to the museum. I hope to take what we have and improve on it with new features, whilst hopefully re-writing a few areas that I found difficult to build, now that my knowledge of Vue has increased.
